About Us

Welcome to
Vaswani Exports

We are leading exporters of Textiles and Food products from India exporting since 1992. Our aim is to provide high-quality, affordable products to our customers around the world. We take great pride in our products and services and are committed to ensuring your satisfaction with every order.

In Textiles we export bleach / dyed / printed fabrics in width 36 inches to 58 inches both in Cotton and Polyester, Shawls and Scarfs. We offer an extensive variety of fabrics.

In Food products we manufacture & export Biscuits, Cookies, Wafers and Rusks. We are exporting Glucose Biscuits, Cream Biscuits, Digestive Biscuits, Salted Biscuits, Crackers, Butter Cookies, Dry Fruit Cookies and Wafer cream Biscuits. We are also manufacturing for many well-known brands in Gulf and African countries.
